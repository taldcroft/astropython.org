Contribute to AstroPython

Contributions and feedback from the community are strongly encouraged. If you simply have a question or comment for the AstroPython team please fill in this form and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

If you have a new resource, question, or any other content that you'd like to post to the web site then likewise fill in this form to send us email and we'll generally post to the site within a day. If the content is relatively short then just include in this form. For longer posts with formatting or web links the preferred method is to just send a message here indicating the topic and we will contact you by email. Generally we'd like to receive a document in simple HTML and we'll take care of posting.

If you are interested in contributing on a more frequent basis then please write and we will create an astropython.org user account which will allow you to log in and post content directly to the site. We hope to one day have a more convenient online registration system or OpenID login system but this is not yet a reality.
